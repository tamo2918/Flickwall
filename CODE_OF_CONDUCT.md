# Code of Conduct

Flickwall aims to be a respectful, practical open source project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct and constructive.
- Focus criticism on code, design, behavior, and maintainability.
- Assume good intent while still asking for evidence and clarity.
- Respect different experience levels and development environments.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory language or behavior
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated off-topic disruption

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, block users, or decline contributions that
make the project hostile or unsafe for contributors and users.
